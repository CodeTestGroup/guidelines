CodeTestGroup Community Guidelines

1. Respect Others

Treat fellow community members with kindness and respect. Everyone comes from different backgrounds and experiences, so it's important to foster a supportive atmosphere. Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior.

2. Be Inclusive

We celebrate diversity and encourage inclusivity. Be mindful of language and actions that may exclude or make others feel unwelcome. Embrace different perspectives and experiences, and help create an environment where everyone feels valued.

8. Stay Informed

Regularly review and stay informed about any updates to the community guidelines. CodeTestGroup may make changes to ensure the continued well-being of the community.

Thank you for being a part of CodeTestGroup! Let's work together to create a supportive and collaborative community for everyone.
